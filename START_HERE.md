## What This Is

Évoche Café — idle/clicker game themed around a café workplace.  
Cookie Clicker-style. Currency = "Covers" (restaurant term).  
Buildings = café stations. Warm espresso/cream/gold pixel-art aesthetic.

Originally built as a minigame inside the buddy-system PWA showcase tab.  
Extracted 2026-05-20 to grow independently as its own standalone PWA.
